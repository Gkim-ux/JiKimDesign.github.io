About

Hi! My name is Jihyun Kim, a graphic designer from Toronto, Canada, currently studying at George Brown College, where I am majoring in graphic design. My journey into this field began in high school during a Careers class, where I chose graphic design for a research project. Having always been passionate about art, I felt an instant connection, and graphic design quickly became my focus.

In my last two years of high school, I dove into various art courses, including visual arts and communication technology. These classes introduced me to essential tools like Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign, providing a strong foundation for my growth as a designer. While the focus then was introductory, these experiences sparked a passion that I’ve been nurturing ever since, driving me to transform my love for design into a meaningful career.
